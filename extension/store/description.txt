AutoApply AI — Smart Resume Assistant for Job Applications

AutoApply AI is your intelligent job application co-pilot. It automatically detects when you're on a job application page, retrieves your best-matching resume from your personal vault, scores it against the job description in real-time, and helps you fill application forms and answer open-ended questions — all without leaving the page.

KEY FEATURES:

🎯 Smart Resume Vault
Upload all your past resumes once. AutoApply AI indexes them with TF-IDF similarity so it always retrieves the right one for each company and role.

📊 ATS Score in Real-Time
See exactly how well your resume matches any job description — keyword coverage, skills gap, quantification score, and actionable suggestions to improve your match rate.

✍️ AI-Powered Q&A Drafts
For open-ended application questions ("Why do you want to work here?", "Tell us about a challenge"), AutoApply AI generates 3 tailored drafts based on your real work history. Pick the best one and save it for future callback reference.

📋 Form Auto-Fill
Detected form fields (name, email, phone, LinkedIn) are highlighted with suggested values. Click Fill to populate them instantly.

🔍 Job Scout Mode
On LinkedIn, Indeed, and Glassdoor job listing pages, see your resume match score for every job on the page — before you even click apply.

📎 Resume Attachment
Attach your tailored resume PDF directly to the file upload field with one click.

🔒 Privacy First
All your data stays in your own private GitHub repository and personal database. No third-party data sharing.

SUPPORTED JOB PLATFORMS:
Greenhouse, Lever, Workday, iCIMS, Taleo, SmartRecruiters, LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, Ashby, Jobvite, and most careers pages.

REQUIREMENTS:
- AutoApply AI backend running (self-hosted or Render free tier)
- Clerk account for authentication (free)
- Optional: Anthropic/OpenAI API key for AI resume generation
